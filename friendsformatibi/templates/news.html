July 23

Jass Queen 2024: Monika Beck

Oliver Müller
/

Gschobe, Obenabe, Nell, Stöck, Bock – that's what it sounded like on July 13 in the garden of the Mettler family. 15 people competed for the title of Jass King. The fun of competing was in the foreground, not winning itself. A wonderfully diverse group - and the non-Jassers had fun with food, conversations and other board and card games. The weather was perfect, 24 degrees, a bit cloudy and dry. So we could sit outside and concentrate fully on our fun.
